Missa Tu es pastor ovium (4vv)

124 MISSA TV ES PARTOR [sic] OVIVM. iiij. vocum. // DIDACVS DE BRVCEÑA. 125
Kyrie (124-125): Superius and Altus complete; significant losses from Tenor, minor losses from Bassus.
Gloria (126-129): Superius and Altus complete; significant losses from Tenor, minor losses from Bassus.
Credo (130-135): Superius and Altus complete; significant losses from Tenor and Bassus.
Sanctus (136-137): Superius and Altus complete; some losses from Tenor and Bassus.
Benedictus (138-139): 3vv (SAT) complete.
Agnus (140-141): complete.
Source motet: Giovanni Pierluigi da Palestrina, Tu es pastor ovium 4vv (SATB), from Motecta festorum totius anni liber primus (Venice: Antonio Gardano, 1564) [RISM P689], no. 14.
